<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Oil Coolant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooled</li><li>Liquid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine oil coolant market comprises two main types: air-cooled and liquid-cooled systems. Air-cooled systems use ambient air to dissipate heat from the engine oil, making them simpler and lighter, often found in smaller vehicles or motorcycles. Liquid-cooled systems circulate a coolant, usually a mixture of water and antifreeze, through the engine to absorb heat more effectively. This method provides superior temperature regulation, leading to better performance and extended engine life, common in larger vehicles and high-performance engines.</p></p>
