<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIR) analyzers utilize light in the near-infrared spectrum to assess the composition of materials. The FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near-Infrared) type employs interferometry to enhance measurement precision by splitting light into multiple wavelengths simultaneously. This method is highly sensitive and provides rapid analysis, making it ideal for applications in agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food processing. FT-NIR analyzers can effectively measure moisture, protein, and fat content in various samples, improving quality control and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
