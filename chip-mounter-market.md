<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology (SMT) Equipment</li><li>Through hole technology (THT) Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip mounter market includes two main types of equipment: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) Equipment and Through-Hole Technology (THT) Equipment. SMT equipment is used for mounting electronic components directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB). This technology is widely popular in the industry for its high speed and efficiency. On the other hand, THT equipment is used for mounting components through holes in the PCB, providing strong mechanical connections. Both types of equipment play important roles in the electronics manufacturing industry.</p></p>
