Solid-state lasers offer reliability and robustness, frequently utilized in industrial and medical settings. Diode lasers are compact and energy-efficient, ideal for telecommunications and consumer electronics. Dye lasers, with tunable wavelengths, are used in spectroscopy, while excimer lasers are prominent in microfabrication and medical procedures.</p></p>
