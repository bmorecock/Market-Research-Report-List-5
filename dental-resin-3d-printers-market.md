<p><p>The dental resin 3D printer market consists of several key technologies. Vat Photopolymerization utilizes a light source to cure liquid resin layer by layer, providing high precision. Fused Deposition Modeling extrudes thermoplastic filaments to build models, suitable for functional parts. PolyJet Technology jets photopolymer droplets, allowing for multi-material and color printing. Selective Laser Sintering fuses powdered resin with lasers for intricate designs. Additional methods, categorized as "Others," may include techniques like material jetting or binder jetting, offering diverse solutions for dental applications.</p></p>
