<p><p>The surgical operation gloves market is divided into two primary types: natural latex gloves and non-latex gloves. Natural latex gloves, made from rubber tree sap, offer excellent elasticity, comfort, and tactile sensitivity, making them a popular choice among healthcare professionals. However, they can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. Non-latex gloves include options made from materials like vinyl, nitrile, and neoprene, providing a suitable alternative for those with latex allergies. These gloves are increasingly preferred for their durability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
