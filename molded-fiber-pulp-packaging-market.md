<p><p>Molded Fiber Pulp Packaging has various types including Thick Wall (Slush Molded), Transfer Molded, Thermoformed Fiber, and Processed Pulp. Thick Wall (Slush Molded) packaging is made by pouring a thick slurry of pulp into a mold. Transfer Molded packaging involves transferring pulp from one mold to another to create the final product. Thermoformed Fiber packaging uses heat and pressure to mold pulp into different shapes. Processed Pulp market involves using advanced technology to create unique and customized pulp packaging solutions.</p></p>
