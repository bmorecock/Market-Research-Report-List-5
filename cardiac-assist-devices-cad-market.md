<p><p>Cardiac Assist Devices (CAD) are medical devices designed to support or replace the function of the heart in patients with severe cardiac conditions. Key types include Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs), which help pump blood from the ventricles to the body; Total Artificial Hearts (TAHs), which replace the entire heart; and Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs), which improve coronary blood flow and reduce the heart's workload during recovery. These devices are crucial for managing heart failure and facilitating heart transplants.</p></p>
