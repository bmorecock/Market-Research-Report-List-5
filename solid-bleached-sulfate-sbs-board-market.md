<p><p>Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) Board is available in two main types - SBS C1S (Coated One Side) and SBS C2S (Coated Two Sides). SBS C1S has a coating on one side, making it suitable for applications where only one side needs to be printed or displayed. On the other hand, SBS C2S has a coating on both sides, offering a high-quality surface on both sides for printing. Both types of SBS boards are commonly used in packaging, publishing, and printing industries.</p></p>
