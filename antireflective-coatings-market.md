<p><p>The antireflective coatings market is primarily categorized based on deposition techniques, including vacuum deposition, electron beam evaporation, and sputtering. Vacuum deposition involves depositing thin layers in a controlled environment to enhance optical performance. Electron beam evaporation uses focused electron beams to vaporize materials, resulting in high-quality coatings. Sputtering involves ejecting material from a target surface onto substrates, ensuring uniform coverage. Each technique offers distinct advantages for various applications, such as optics, electronics, and solar cells, driving market growth.</p></p>
