<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Biopesticide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li><li>Plant-Incorporated Protectants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopesticide market is segmented into four main types: Microbial pesticides, which utilize naturally occurring microorganisms to control pests; Biochemical pesticides, derived from natural materials like plants, animals, or minerals, offering low toxicity; Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs), genetically engineered plants that produce pest-resistant proteins; and Others, which encompasses newer or less common biopesticide technologies. These categories support sustainable agriculture by providing environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional chemical pesticides, promoting ecological balance and safety in pest management.</p></p>
