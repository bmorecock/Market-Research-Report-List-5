<p><p>The wall mount humidity controller market is divided into two main types: automatic and toggle controllers. Automatic controllers use sensors to monitor humidity levels continuously and adjust settings accordingly, providing precise and hassle-free management of indoor environments. In contrast, toggle controllers operate with manual switches, allowing users to set their preferred humidity levels manually. Both types cater to different user preferences, with automatic models offering convenience and efficiency, while toggle controllers provide a more hands-on approach for users who prefer direct control.</p></p>
