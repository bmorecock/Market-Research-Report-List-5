<p><p>Elemental Boron is classified into two main market types: Amorphous Boron and Crystalline Boron. Amorphous Boron is a non-crystalline form of boron, known for its high chemical reactivity and wide range of applications in industries such as agriculture, aerospace, and healthcare. On the other hand, Crystalline Boron is a highly pure and well-structured form of boron, used primarily in advanced materials and semiconductor industries. Both types of boron offer distinct properties and benefits, catering to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
