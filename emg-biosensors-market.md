<p><strong>The EMG Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Biosensors</li><li>Intramuscular Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMG biosensors are classified into two main types: surface biosensors and intramuscular biosensors. Surface biosensors are non-invasive devices placed on the skin to detect electrical signals from muscles, making them ideal for rehabilitation and prosthetic control. Intramuscular biosensors, on the other hand, involve inserting electrodes into the muscle tissue, offering higher precision and sensitivity for applications in clinical diagnostics and advanced research. Both types play crucial roles in medical monitoring and bioengineering, catering to different needs in muscle signal analysis.</p></p>
