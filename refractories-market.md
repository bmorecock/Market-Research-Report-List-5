<p><strong>The Refractories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractories market is divided into two main types - Shaped Refractories and Unshaped Refractories. Shaped Refractories are pre-formed products like bricks, blocks, and shapes that are used to line high-temperature equipment such as furnaces and kilns. On the other hand, Unshaped Refractories are monolithic materials like castables, plastics, and ramming mixes that are applied in a wet state and then hardened to form a lining. Both types play a crucial role in providing thermal insulation and resistance to high temperatures in industrial applications.</p></p>
