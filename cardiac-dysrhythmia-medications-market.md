<p><strong>The Cardiac Dysrhythmia Medications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral Antiarrhythmic</li><li>Intravenous Antiarryhthmic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac dysrhythmia medications can be categorized into two main types - oral antiarrhythmic medications and intravenous antiarrhythmic medications. Oral antiarrhythmic medications are taken orally in the form of pills or capsules and are used for long-term management of cardiac dysrhythmias. On the other hand, intravenous antiarrhythmic medications are administered through injections directly into the bloodstream and are typically used in emergency situations or for patients who are unable to take oral medications. Both types of medications work to regulate the heart's rhythm and prevent abnormal heartbeats.</p></p>
