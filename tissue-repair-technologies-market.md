<p><p>Tissue repair technologies encompass two primary market types: tissue regeneration and tissue replacement. Tissue regeneration focuses on stimulating the body's natural healing processes to restore damaged tissues using methods like stem cells, growth factors, or scaffolds that promote cell growth and tissue formation. In contrast, tissue replacement involves the use of artificial implants or biomaterials to substitute lost or damaged tissues, providing structural and functional support. Both approaches aim to enhance healing outcomes, improve quality of life, and reduce healthcare costs.</p></p>
