<p><p>Epitaxy deposition is a critical process used to create thin films of crystals on substrates in semiconductor manufacturing. The market is primarily categorized into three types: MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition), which utilizes metal-organic compounds for high-quality layer growth; Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE), a technique that deposits materials layer by layer in a vacuum for precise control; and Other CVD Epitaxy methods, which encompass various chemical vapor processes to achieve desired film properties. Each type serves specific applications in electronics and optoelectronics.</p></p>
