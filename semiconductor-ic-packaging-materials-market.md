<p><p>The semiconductor and IC packaging materials market encompasses various types essential for ensuring reliable electronic performance. Organic substrates, made from polymeric materials, provide a lightweight and flexible base for ICs. Bonding wires are thin metal wires used to make electrical connections within the package. Lead frames serve as a conductive framework to facilitate the integration of chips with external circuits. Ceramic packages, known for their superior thermal stability and protection, are utilized in high-performance and demanding applications, ensuring durability and reliability.</p></p>
