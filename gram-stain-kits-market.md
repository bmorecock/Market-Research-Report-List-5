<p><strong>The Gram Stain Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gram's Crystal Violet Solution</li><li>Gram's Iodine Solution</li><li>Gram's Decolorizer Solution</li><li>Gram's Safranin Solution</li><li>Gram's Tartrazine Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gram Stain Kits market encompasses various solutions essential for bacterial classification. Gram's Crystal Violet Solution serves as the primary stain, highlighting Gram-positive bacteria. Gram's Iodine Solution acts as a mordant, enhancing crystal violet retention. Gram's Decolorizer Solution differentiates bacterial types by removing excess dye from Gram-negative cells. Finally, Gram's Safranin Solution serves as a counterstain, coloring decolorized Gram-negative bacteria for visibility. Gram's Tartrazine Solution is less common but may be used for specific staining applications within research and diagnostics.</p></p>
