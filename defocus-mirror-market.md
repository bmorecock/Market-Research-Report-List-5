<p><p>The defocus mirror market encompasses two main types: peripheral defocus and multipoint defocus. Peripheral defocus mirrors create a blurred vision at the edges of the visual field, often utilized in applications requiring a wider field of view. In contrast, multipoint defocus mirrors facilitate focus adjustments at multiple specific points, enhancing visual accuracy and clarity across various distances. Both technologies aim to optimize visual performance and comfort in optical devices, catering to diverse user needs in fields like automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
