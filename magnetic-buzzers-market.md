<p><p>The magnetic buzzers market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive magnetic buzzers. Active magnetic buzzers are self-driven, requiring only a power source to produce sound, making them easy to integrate into electronic systems. In contrast, passive magnetic buzzers need an external audio signal to generate sound, offering more versatility in tone and pitch but relying on additional components. Both types are widely used in applications like alarms, notifications, and consumer electronics, catering to various sound generation needs.</p></p>
