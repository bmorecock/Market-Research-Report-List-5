<p><p>The Transformer and Inductor market utilizing ferrite cores includes various types based on material composition. Manganese Zinc (MnZn) ferrite cores are known for their high permeability and low core losses, making them ideal for power transformers. Nickel Zinc (NiZn) cores excel in high-frequency applications due to lower losses. Sand dust cores offer a cost-effective alternative with good performance in high-frequency applications. Lamination, amorphous, and nanocrystalline cores provide superior efficiency in reducing eddy current losses, enhancing overall performance in transformers and inductors.</p></p>
