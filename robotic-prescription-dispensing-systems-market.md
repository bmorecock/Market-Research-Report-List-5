<p><p>Robotic Prescription Dispensing Systems are mainly classified into Centralized Dispensing Systems and Decentralized Dispensing Systems. Centralized systems involve a single location where medications are stored and dispensed, typically utilized by hospitals and large pharmacies. On the other hand, Decentralized systems involve multiple smaller dispensing units spread out in various locations, such as retail pharmacies or clinics. Both systems aim to increase efficiency, accuracy, and safety in medication dispensing while catering to the specific needs and scale of the healthcare setting.</p></p>
