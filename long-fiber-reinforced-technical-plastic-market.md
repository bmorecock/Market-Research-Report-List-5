LFT-PA (Long Fiber-reinforced Polyamide) offers enhanced thermal stability and mechanical properties. LFT-ABS (Long Fiber-reinforced Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) combines strength with good impact resistance. Other types may include variations using different polymers or composite materials, catering to specific industry needs for performance, durability, and processing characteristics.</p></p>
