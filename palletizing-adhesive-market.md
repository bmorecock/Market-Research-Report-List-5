<p><p>The palletizing adhesive market comprises primarily two types: hot melt palletizing adhesive and spray palletizing adhesive. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic compounds that bond by cooling, offering quick setting times and high strength for packaging applications. They are commonly used in production lines for their efficiency and ease of use. Spray palletizing adhesives, on the other hand, are applied as a fine mist, providing even coverage and flexibility in bonding various materials, making them suitable for diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
