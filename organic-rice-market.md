<p><strong>The Organic Rice Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polished glutinous rice(sticky rice)</li><li>Indica(long-shaped rice)</li><li>Polished round-grained rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic rice market encompasses various types, each catering to specific culinary needs. Polished glutinous rice, also known as sticky rice, is prized for its stickiness when cooked, making it ideal for desserts and Asian dishes. Indica rice, characterized by its long grain, is often favored for its fluffy texture and is commonly used in biryanis and pilafs. Polished round-grained rice, with its plump, soft grains, is popular in risottos and sushi, appealing to diverse consumer preferences in the organic food sector.</p></p>
