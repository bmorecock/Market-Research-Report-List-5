<p><p>A Vehicle Cabin Air Quality Sensor is a device designed to monitor and assess the air quality inside a vehicle's cabin. The sensor helps improve passenger comfort and health by enabling automatic adjustments to the vehicle's ventilation system, ensuring cleaner air circulation.</p><p>The Vehicle Cabin Air Quality Sensor Market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing awareness of air pollution and its health impacts, coupled with rising demand for advanced automotive technologies. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.9% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in sensor technology, such as miniaturization and improved accuracy, are further propelling market expansion. The increasing focus on autonomous vehicles and connected car technologies is also contributing to the demand for cabin air quality sensors, as these systems prioritize passenger well-being and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
