<p><p>Non Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) sensing is a technology used to detect and measure specific gases by identifying their unique infrared absorption characteristics. Unlike dispersive methods that separate infrared light into its components, NDIR sensors allow the targeted gas molecules to absorb specific wavelengths of infrared light, enabling precise concentration measurements. These sensors are widely utilized in applications such as environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and medical diagnostics due to their reliability and accuracy.</p><p>The Non Dispersive Infrared Sensing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.70% during the forecast period.
