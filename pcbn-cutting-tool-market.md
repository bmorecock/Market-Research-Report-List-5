<p><ul><li>Solid Type</li><li>Indexable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PcBN (Polycrystalline Cubic Boron Nitride) cutting tool market is primarily divided into two types: solid and indexable. Solid type tools consist of a single piece of PcBN material, offering high cutting performance for specific applications, particularly in machining hardened steel and nickel alloys. In contrast, indexable type tools feature replaceable inserts, providing flexibility and reduced tool-change times for various machining operations. Both types are valued for their durability, thermal stability, and effectiveness in enhancing productivity in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
