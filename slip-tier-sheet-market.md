<p><p>Slip and tier sheets are essential components in logistics and packaging, designed to facilitate the stacking and storage of products. Slip sheets are thin sheets used to support loads and enable easy movement with forklifts, often made from cardboard or plastic. Tier sheets, on the other hand, are thicker sheets placed between product layers to prevent damage during transit and enhance stability. Both types play a vital role in optimizing space utilization and improving handling efficiency in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
