<p><p>The signal jammer market can be categorized into two main types: Specific/Single Signal Jammers and Multi-Band/Channel Signal Jammers. Specific jammers are designed to disrupt a single frequency band, targeting specific communications like mobile phones or Wi-Fi. In contrast, multi-band jammers can interfere with multiple frequency bands simultaneously, providing broader coverage against various devices. This versatility makes multi-band jammers preferable for extensive applications, while specific jammers are often used for targeted scenarios, ensuring efficient disruption based on user needs.</p></p>
