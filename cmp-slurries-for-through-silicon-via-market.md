<p><ul><li>Front Side Slurries</li><li>Back Side Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential for polishing silicon wafers in semiconductor manufacturing, particularly for Through Silicon Via (TSV) structures. Front side slurries are used to planarize the top layer of silicon, ensuring a smooth surface for subsequent layer applications. In contrast, back side slurries are utilized for polishing the rear side of the wafer, facilitating effective TSV creation and enhancing chip performance. Both types optimize wafer flatness and achieve desired surface qualities for advanced packaging applications.</p></p>
