<p><p>Sodium PCA is a natural moisturizing factor that is commonly used in skincare products. It is available in two different forms in the market - L-form and DL-form. The L-form is the active form of Sodium PCA that is naturally found in the skin and is more bioavailable. On the other hand, the DL-form is a racemic mixture of L and D forms, which is less effective than the L-form. Both forms are popular in the cosmetic industry for their hydrating and skin conditioning properties.</p></p>
