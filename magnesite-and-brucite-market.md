<p><strong>The Magnesite and Brucite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phanerocrystalline magnesite</li><li>Cryptocrystalline magnesite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesite and brucite markets are categorized based on their crystal structures. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features larger, visible crystals and is primarily used in refractory materials and steelmaking due to its high thermal stability. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite consists of finely crystalline structures that offer different physical properties, making it suitable for commercial applications like fertilizers and as a flux in glass manufacturing. Brucite, a magnesium hydroxide mineral, also serves various industries, including environmental applications and flame retardants, enhancing its market relevance.</p></p>
