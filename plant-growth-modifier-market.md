<p><p>The Plant Growth Modifier market is categorized into several types, including Auxins, Cytokinins, Gibberellins, and Others. Auxins promote root development and are critical for cell elongation. Cytokinins stimulate cell division and play a vital role in shoot growth. Gibberellins affect seed germination and enhance flowering and fruit development. The "Other" category encompasses various lesser-known growth regulators that support diverse plant development processes. These modifiers are essential for optimizing crop yield and improving agricultural productivity.</p></p>
