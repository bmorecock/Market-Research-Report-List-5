<p><ul><li>Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM)</li><li>Optical Digitizer and Scanner (ODS)</li><li>Video Measuring Machine (VMM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Metrology Instrument market encompasses several key types, including Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM), Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODS), and Video Measuring Machines (VMM). CMMs utilize precise probes to measure the physical geometrical characteristics of an object, enabling high accuracy in quality control. ODS employs laser or light-based technologies for digitizing physical objects into 3D models, enhancing design and inspection processes. VMMs utilize video-based systems to capture measurements from parts, providing rapid and effective dimensional analysis for manufacturing applications.</p></p>
