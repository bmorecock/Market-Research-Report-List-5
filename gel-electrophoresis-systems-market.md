<p><strong>The Gel Electrophoresis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li><li>Vertical Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gel electrophoresis systems market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical gel electrophoresis systems. Horizontal gel electrophoresis systems are primarily used for separating nucleic acids, such as DNA and RNA, across agarose gels, providing a user-friendly setup for routine applications. In contrast, vertical gel electrophoresis systems are designed for protein separation using polyacrylamide gels, allowing for more complex analysis and higher resolution. Both types serve distinct purposes in laboratories, catering to diverse research and diagnostic needs in molecular biology and biochemistry.</p></p>
