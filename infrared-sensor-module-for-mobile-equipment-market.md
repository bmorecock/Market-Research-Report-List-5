<p><p>Infrared sensor modules are essential components in mobile equipment for detecting radiation emitted by objects. There are two main types of infrared sensors used in mobile equipment: Uncooled Infrared (UIR) sensors and Cooled Infrared (CIR) sensors. UIR sensors do not require cooling mechanisms, making them more cost-effective and compact, while CIR sensors utilize cooling technology for higher sensitivity and accuracy in detecting infrared radiation. Both UIR and CIR sensors play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and functionality of mobile equipment.</p></p>
