<p><strong>The Shotcrete Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Shotcrete Machines</li><li>Dry Shotcrete Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shotcrete machines market is divided into two main types: wet shotcrete machines and dry shotcrete machines. Wet shotcrete machines mix water with the cementitious material before application, offering better adhesion and reduced dust. They are ideal for large-scale projects requiring high volumes. In contrast, dry shotcrete machines deliver dry material that is mixed with water at the nozzle, allowing for flexibility in application and easier transport. Both types serve different needs in construction, mining, and tunneling applications.</p></p>
