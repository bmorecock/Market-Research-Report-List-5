<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum gratings market is primarily categorized into press-locked and swage-locked gratings. Press-locked gratings feature a design where the crossbars are pressed into the bearing bars, creating a strong and stable structure ideal for heavy loads. Swage-locked gratings, on the other hand, involve crossbars that are swaged into bearing bars, providing an even more robust connection. Both types are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for various applications, including walkways, platforms, and industrial flooring.</p></p>
