<p><p>The inhaled medications market encompasses various device types designed for efficient drug delivery to the lungs. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) utilize a powdered form of medication activated by the user's inhalation force. Metered-Dose Inhalers (MDIs) dispense a predetermined dose of medication in aerosol form with each press of a canister. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation, ideal for patients with difficulty using other devices. Soft Mist Inhalers deliver medication in a slow-moving mist, enhancing drug deposition in the lungs.</p></p>
