<p><p>The Ultra-Wideband (UWB) market can be categorized into two main types: Impulse Radio and Multi-band UWB. Impulse Radio uses short, low-power pulses to transmit data over a broad frequency range, enabling high-precision ranging and localization applications. In contrast, Multi-band UWB combines several frequency bands to achieve high data rates, making it suitable for robust communication systems. Both types leverage UWB's unique characteristics for applications like wireless sensor networks, healthcare monitoring, and automotive safety, enhancing efficiency and connectivity.</p></p>
