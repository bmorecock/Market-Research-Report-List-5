<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>G-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photolithography agent market consists of several types, including ArF immersion, ArF dry, KrF, I-Line, and G-Line technologies. ArF immersion uses a liquid medium to enhance resolution, suitable for advanced chips. ArF dry employs gases for light exposure, mainly in high-volume production. KrF technology operates at shorter wavelengths for medium-resolution patterns. I-Line is used for lower-resolution applications, while G-Line targets older semiconductor processes. Each type serves specific needs in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing production efficiency and device performance.</p></p>
