<p><p>The industrial metal cleaners market primarily consists of alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners, each serving distinct purposes. Alkaline cleaners, typically used for removing oils, greases, and particulate residues, operate effectively in various industrial applications, providing excellent cleaning power without harming metal substrates. Conversely, acid cleaners are tailored for removing rust, scale, and oxides; they are particularly effective on specific metals but require careful handling due to their corrosive nature. Together, these cleaners play vital roles in maintaining metal surfaces in industrial settings.</p></p>
