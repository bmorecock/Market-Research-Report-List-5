<p><p>The off-road vehicle HID lamps market is segmented into two types: built-in ballast and external ballast. Built-in ballast HID lamps integrate the ballast within the lamp housing, providing a compact design that simplifies installation and reduces space requirements. In contrast, external ballast HID lamps have the ballast separate from the lamp, allowing for more flexibility in installation and potentially better heat management. Both types offer enhanced illumination and durability suited for rugged off-road conditions.</p></p>
