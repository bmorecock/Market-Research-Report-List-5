<p><p>The benchtop water analysis instrumentation market encompasses two primary types: physical assays and chemical assays. Physical assays focus on measuring properties such as turbidity, conductivity, and temperature, providing insights into the water's physical characteristics. In contrast, chemical assays analyze water samples for specific contaminants and chemical concentrations, helping to assess water quality and safety. Both types are essential for applications in environmental monitoring, water treatment, and compliance testing, ensuring reliable and accurate water analysis for various industries.</p></p>
