<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-etch adhesive market is divided into two main types: single component and two component adhesives. Single component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, simplifying application and reducing the risk of errors. They often offer convenience and consistent performance. In contrast, two component adhesives involve mixing two separate components, typically a resin and a hardener, which can enhance bond strength and tailor properties for specific applications. Both types cater to diverse needs in various industries, including construction and automotive.</p></p>
