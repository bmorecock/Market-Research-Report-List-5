<p><p>The biostimulant market is categorized into three main types: acid-based, extract-based, and others. Acid-based biostimulants typically include substances like humic and fulvic acids, which enhance nutrient uptake and soil health. Extract-based biostimulants are derived from natural sources such as seaweed, plants, and microbial resources, promoting growth and resilience. The "others" category encompasses novel formulations and synthetic compounds that may not fit into the first two types but still contribute to plant health and productivity.</p></p>
