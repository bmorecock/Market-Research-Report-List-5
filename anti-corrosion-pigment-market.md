<p><strong>The Anti-corrosion Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosphates Based</li><li>Silica Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-corrosion pigment market is categorized into phosphates-based and silica-based types. Phosphates-based pigments, known for their excellent protective properties, inhibit corrosion by forming a stable barrier and promoting passivation on metal surfaces. Silica-based pigments, on the other hand, provide durability and thermal stability, enhancing the effectiveness of coatings while improving their mechanical strength. Both types are crucial in various industries, including automotive and construction, to ensure longevity and protection of metal structures against environmental factors.</p></p>
