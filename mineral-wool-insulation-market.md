<p><p>The mineral wool insulation market primarily includes two types: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool is made from recycled glass and is known for its lightweight, soundproofing, and thermal insulation properties, making it ideal for residential and commercial applications. Stone wool, derived from volcanic rock or basalt, provides excellent fire resistance and moisture control, making it suitable for industrial and high-temperature settings. Both types offer energy efficiency and sustainability benefits, contributing to their growing demand in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
