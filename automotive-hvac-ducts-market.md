<p><p>The automotive HVAC ducts market comprises two main types: gas ducts and liquid ducts. Gas ducts transport heated or cooled air throughout the vehicle, ensuring passenger comfort and optimal climate control. They are typically designed for efficient airflow and minimal noise. In contrast, liquid ducts are primarily used for coolant or fluid distribution, managing the temperature of engine components and enhancing overall thermal performance. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining a comfortable and efficient driving environment.</p></p>
