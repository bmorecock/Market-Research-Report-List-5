<p><p>The fumed silica market is categorized based on the specific surface area, measured in Brunauer-Emmett-Teller (BET) values. BET 100-160 refers to low surface area fumed silica, used for enhancing flowability and reducing viscosity in applications like coatings and adhesives. BET 160-210 offers medium surface area for improved absorption and thickening in formulations. BET 210-300 is high surface area silica, ideal for reinforcement in rubber and plastics. The "Others" category includes specialized grades tailored for niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
