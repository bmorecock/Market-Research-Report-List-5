<p><p>The plastic fencing market features various types, each with unique properties. Vinyl fencing is durable, low-maintenance, and resistant to rotting. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its strength and UV resistance, making it suitable for outdoor use. Wood-plastic composites (WPC) combine wood fibers with plastic, offering a natural appearance with enhanced durability. Polystyrene is lightweight and versatile, often used for decorative panels. Polyethylene fencing is flexible and resistant to weather, making it ideal for various applications.</p></p>
