<p><p>Tackifier resin dispersions are categorized into three main types: Rosin Ester Series, Terpene Phenolic Resin Series, and Polymeric Rosin. Rosin Ester Series offers strong adhesion and is commonly used in pressure-sensitive adhesives. Terpene Phenolic Resin Series provides excellent thermal stability and is favored in high-performance applications. Polymeric Rosin is valued for its versatility and is used in diverse formulations, enhancing adhesion properties and adjusting viscosity in adhesives, sealants, and coatings across various industries.</p></p>
