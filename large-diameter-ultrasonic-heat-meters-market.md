<p><p>Large diameter ultrasonic heat meters are classified into integrated and split heat meters. Integrated heat meters combine all components, including flow sensors and measurement units, into a single device, simplifying installation and maintenance. In contrast, split heat meters separate the sensor units from the display and processing equipment, allowing for flexible installation in challenging environments. Both types utilize ultrasonic technology for precise measurement of heat energy in large systems, catering to various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
