<p><strong>The Alcohol-free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market is primarily divided into two types based on production methods: fermentation-limited and dealcoholization. The fermentation-limited method involves controlling fermentation to restrict alcohol formation, yielding a beverage with minimal alcohol content. In contrast, the dealcoholization method starts with regular beer, which is then processed to remove the alcohol, ensuring the retention of flavor and aroma while achieving low or zero alcohol levels. Both methods cater to growing health-conscious consumer demand for non-alcoholic options.</p></p>
