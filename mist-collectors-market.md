<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Electrostatic</li><li>Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mist collectors are essential for removing airborne particulate in industrial settings and come in three main market types. Centrifugal mist collectors utilize high-speed rotation to separate droplets from the air, effectively capturing larger particles. Electrostatic mist collectors employ electrostatic charges to attract and collect finer mist particles. Media-based mist collectors utilize filters or mesh to physically trap contaminants as air passes through. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the application, efficiency requirements, and specific environmental conditions.</p></p>
