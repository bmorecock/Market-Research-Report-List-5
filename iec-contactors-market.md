<p><strong>The IEC Contactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive</li><li>Reverse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IEC contactor market can be categorized into positive and reverse types. Positive contactors are designed for applications requiring safe and reliable switching to ensure continuous operation. They facilitate a straightforward and uninterrupted electrical supply. On the other hand, reverse contactors enable reversible motor control, allowing motors to rotate in both directions. This is crucial for applications like conveyor belts and elevators, where direction change is necessary. Together, these contactor types support a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
