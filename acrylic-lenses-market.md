<p><p>The acrylic lenses market primarily consists of two main types: Monofocal Intraocular Lenses (IOLs) and Premium IOLs. Monofocal IOLs provide a single corrective focus, typically for distance vision, making them a cost-effective option for cataract surgery. In contrast, Premium IOLs offer advanced features, such as multifocal or accommodating capabilities, enabling improved vision at multiple distances. This segment appeals to patients seeking enhanced visual outcomes and quality of life post-surgery, driving growth in the premium lens market.</p></p>
