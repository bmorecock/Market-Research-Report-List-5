<p><p>The Transvenous Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Biventricular ICDs** (also known as Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators) improve heart efficiency by coordinating contractions of both ventricles. </p><p>2. **Dual-Chamber ICDs** monitor and regulate the heart's atrium and ventricle, ensuring a coordinated rhythm. </p><p>3. **Single-Chamber ICDs** are simpler devices focused on the ventricle, providing effective defibrillation in patients with less complex arrhythmia needs. Each type is tailored to specific patient conditions and requirements.</p></p>
