<p><strong>The Glass FIber Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass fiber nonwoven market can be divided into two main types: wet-laid process and dry-laid process. Wet-laid process involves forming a mat from glass fibers in a water suspension, followed by draining and drying. This method ensures uniformity and high tensile strength. On the other hand, dry-laid process involves assembling glass fibers into a mat through air or mechanical means, followed by binding with a resin or binder. This method is quicker and less expensive, making it suitable for high-volume production.</p></p>
