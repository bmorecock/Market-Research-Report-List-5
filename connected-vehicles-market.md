<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Connected Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Connected Vehicles</li><li>Tethered Connected Vehicles</li><li>Integrated Connected Vehicles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Connected Vehicles Market** comprises three main types:  </p><p>1. **Embedded Connected Vehicles**: These have built-in connectivity hardware and software, enabling direct communication with networks, other vehicles, and infrastructure without external devices.  </p><p>2. **Tethered Connected Vehicles**: These rely on external devices like smartphones for connectivity, leveraging the device's network and apps to enable connected features.  </p><p>3. **Integrated Connected Vehicles**: These combine embedded and tethered systems, offering seamless connectivity through both built-in hardware and external devices, enhancing flexibility and functionality.</p></p>
