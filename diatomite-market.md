<p><p>The diatomite market is categorized into two main types: anhydrous substance and flux calcined. Anhydrous diatomite consists of pure, unprocessed diatomaceous earth primarily used in filtration and insulation applications. Flux calcined diatomite, on the other hand, is treated with high temperatures to enhance its properties, making it suitable for purposes like ceramics and metal casting. Both types leverage the unique porous structure and absorbent qualities of diatomite, catering to diverse industrial needs across various sectors.</p></p>
