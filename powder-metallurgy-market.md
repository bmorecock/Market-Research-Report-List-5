<p><ul><li>Ferrous</li><li>Non-ferrous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder Metallurgy market can be broadly classified into two main categories: Ferrous and Non-ferrous. Ferrous powder metallurgy involves the production of metal components from iron-based powders, including materials like iron, steel, and stainless steel. On the other hand, Non-ferrous powder metallurgy encompasses the manufacturing of components using powders of metals other than iron, such as aluminum, copper, nickel, and titanium. Both Ferrous and Non-ferrous powder metallurgy processes offer cost-effective and efficient solutions for producing complex parts with high precision and material properties.</p></p>
