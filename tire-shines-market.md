<p><strong>The Tire Shines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Tire Shine</li><li>Silicone-Based Tire Shine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire shine market includes water-based and silicone-based products. Water-based tire shines are eco-friendly, non-greasy, and provide a natural, matte finish. They are easy to apply, dry quickly, and are less likely to attract dust. Silicone-based tire shines offer a high-gloss, wet-look finish and longer-lasting protection. However, they can be greasy, attract dirt, and may harm rubber over time. Both types cater to different preferences, with water-based options appealing to eco-conscious users and silicone-based products targeting those seeking a durable, glossy shine.</p></p>
