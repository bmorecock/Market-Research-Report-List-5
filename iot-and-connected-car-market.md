<p><p>The IoT and Connected Car market encompasses various communication types that enhance vehicle connectivity. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) facilitates direct communication between vehicles to share data on speed, direction, and hazards. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) connects vehicles with traffic signals and road signs for improved navigation. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) promotes safety by allowing cars to communicate with nearby pedestrians and cyclists. V2N (Vehicle-to-Network) enables vehicles to connect to cellular networks for real-time data access and cloud services, enhancing overall driving experience and safety.</p></p>
