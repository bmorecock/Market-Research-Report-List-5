<p><p>The Alzheimer's Disease drug market includes several key types: Donepezil, Memantine, and Rivastigmine. Donepezil is a cholinesterase inhibitor used to enhance cognitive functions in mild to moderate Alzheimer's patients. Memantine, an NMDA receptor antagonist, helps improve memory and daily functioning in moderate to severe cases. Rivastigmine, also a cholinesterase inhibitor, is available in oral and transdermal forms and addresses both mild and moderate stages. Together, these medications aim to slow disease progression and improve quality of life for patients.</p></p>
