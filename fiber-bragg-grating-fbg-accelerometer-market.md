<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) accelerometer market is segmented into two main types: mono-axial and multi-axial accelerometers. Mono-axial FBG accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, making them suitable for applications where directional sensitivity is critical. In contrast, multi-axial FBG accelerometers can measure acceleration across multiple axes simultaneously, offering a comprehensive solution for dynamic monitoring in complex environments. Both types leverage the unique properties of fiber optics, providing high sensitivity and resilience in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
