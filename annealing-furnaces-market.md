<p><p>The annealing furnaces market is categorized into batch and continuous types. Batch type furnaces process multiple loads simultaneously, allowing for flexibility and precise control over temperature and time, ideal for smaller production runs or varied materials. In contrast, continuous type furnaces facilitate a seamless flow of materials, enhancing efficiency for high-volume operations. These systems maintain consistent temperatures, reducing cycle times and energy consumption, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing applications where uniformity and throughput are critical.</p></p>
