<p><strong>The Medical Surgical Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Medical Surgical Masks</li><li>Non-woven Medical Surgical Masks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical surgical mask market comprises two main types: woven and non-woven masks. Woven medical surgical masks are made from interlaced fibers, providing a good barrier but may offer limited fluid resistance. In contrast, non-woven medical surgical masks are crafted from synthetic materials, such as polypropylene, which enhance filtration efficiency and fluid resistance, making them suitable for high-risk environments. Both mask types play critical roles in infection control within healthcare settings, with non-woven masks typically being the preferred choice.</p></p>
