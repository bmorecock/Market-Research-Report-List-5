<p><p>The water repellent market comprises three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and silicone-based products. Water-based repellents utilize water as a solvent and are eco-friendly, suitable for various substrates. Solvent-based repellents contain organic solvents, providing stronger moisture protection but may have environmental concerns. Silicone-based repellents offer excellent durability and flexibility, forming a protective barrier on surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to different performance needs and environmental considerations in industries such as construction, textiles, and automotive.</p></p>
