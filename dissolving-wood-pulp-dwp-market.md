<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dissolving Wood Pulp (DWP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Wood Pulp Or Sulfate Wood Pulp</li><li>Wood Pulp Of Sulfite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolving Wood Pulp (DWP) is an intermediate product used to produce cellulose derivatives for various industries, including textiles and pharmaceuticals. The market types include Alkali Wood Pulp or Sulfate Wood Pulp, which involves the kraft process, utilizing chemicals to break down lignin, yielding stronger fibers. Wood Pulp of Sulfite, on the other hand, employs sulfite chemicals, resulting in a softer pulp ideal for certain applications. Each type offers unique properties suited for specific end-use products in the cellulose-based market.</p></p>
