<p><p>The Snow Sweeper Truck market features two primary types: Rotary and Wedge Snow Sweeper Trucks. Rotary Snow Sweepers utilize rotating brushes to efficiently clear snow from roads and surfaces, making them suitable for larger areas and heavy snowfall conditions. In contrast, Wedge Snow Sweepers leverage a wedge-shaped design to push snow aside while sweeping, offering precision in narrow spaces and urban environments. Both types are essential for effective winter maintenance, enhancing road safety and accessibility during snow events.</p></p>
