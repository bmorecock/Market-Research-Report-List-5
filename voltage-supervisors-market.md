<p><strong>The Voltage Supervisors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mounting</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage supervisors market is categorized into two main types: surface mount and through-hole. Surface mount voltage supervisors are compact and designed for easy integration onto printed circuit boards (PCBs), making them ideal for modern, space-constrained applications. In contrast, through-hole voltage supervisors feature leads that pass through the PCB, offering robust connections and are typically used in applications requiring durability. Each type serves specific design needs, influencing factors such as assembly technique, space efficiency, and mechanical stability.</p></p>
