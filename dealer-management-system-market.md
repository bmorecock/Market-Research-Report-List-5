<p><p>Dealer Management Systems (DMS) are categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud-based solutions. On-Premise DMS is installed locally on a dealer's hardware, providing greater control and security but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-based DMS operates over the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier updates with lower initial costs. Businesses can choose between these types based on their operational needs, budget, and preferred level of control over their data.</p></p>
