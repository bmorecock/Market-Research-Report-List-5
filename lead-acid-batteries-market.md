<p><p>Lead-Acid Batteries are commonly categorized into two main types - Valve Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) Battery and Flooded Battery. VRLA batteries are maintenance-free and sealed, making them ideal for applications where regular maintenance is difficult. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance such as adding water to the cells. The market for VRLA batteries is growing due to their convenience and versatility, while flooded batteries are still used in applications where cost is a major consideration.</p></p>
