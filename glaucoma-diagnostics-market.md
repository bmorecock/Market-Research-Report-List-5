<p><strong>The Glaucoma Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Low Tension Glaucoma</li><li>Pigmentary Glaucoma</li><li>Closed Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma diagnostics market comprises various types, each associated with distinct characteristics. Open Angle Glaucoma, the most common form, often progresses unnoticed, requiring regular pressure monitoring. Low Tension Glaucoma presents normal eye pressure despite optic nerve damage. Pigmentary Glaucoma involves pigment dispersion affecting intraocular pressure. Closed Angle Glaucoma results from the blockage of fluid drainage in the eye, leading to sudden symptoms. Other types may include congenital or secondary glaucomas, representing a diverse range of conditions requiring specialized diagnostic approaches.</p></p>
