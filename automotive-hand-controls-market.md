<p><p>The Automotive Hand Controls Market includes various types designed for drivers with limited mobility. Push-Pull Hand Controls allow acceleration and braking through forward and backward movements. Push Right Angle Hand Controls use a perpendicular lever for similar functions, offering a different ergonomic approach. Push-Rock Hand Controls combine pushing for acceleration and rocking for braking, providing intuitive operation. Push-Twist Hand Controls integrate twisting motions for acceleration and braking, ensuring precise control. Each type caters to diverse needs, enhancing accessibility and driving comfort for users with physical challenges.</p></p>
