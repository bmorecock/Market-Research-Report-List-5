<p><p>The Carbon Elemental Analyzer market consists of various types, primarily including combustion analysis and spectral analysis. Combustion analysis involves burning samples to measure carbon content through gas emissions, providing accurate quantification of carbon in organic materials. Spectral analysis, on the other hand, utilizes techniques like infrared or Raman spectroscopy to determine carbon presence based on light absorption or scattering. Other methods may include thermal analysis and elemental mass spectrometry, catering to diverse analytical needs across industries such as environmental monitoring and material science.</p></p>
