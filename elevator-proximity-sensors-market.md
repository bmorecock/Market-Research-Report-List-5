<p><p>Elevator proximity sensors enhance safety and efficiency in lift operations by detecting the presence of individuals near doors. Multi-beam door sensors employ multiple infrared beams to sense obstructions, ensuring doors remain open until the pathway is clear. Ultrasonic door sensors utilize sound waves to detect movement, allowing for quick and accurate detection of people approaching the elevator. Both technologies aim to prevent accidents and improve user convenience, playing a crucial role in modern elevator systems.</p></p>
