<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water treatment equipment market includes Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU systems are installed at specific water outlets, like faucets or showerheads, to treat water at the point of use. POE systems, on the other hand, treat water as it enters the household, providing clean water throughout the entire home. POU systems are popular for targeted water treatment needs, while POE systems are preferred for overall water quality improvement. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and clean drinking water for households.</p></p>
