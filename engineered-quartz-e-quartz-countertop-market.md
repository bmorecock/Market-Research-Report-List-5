<p><p>The engineered quartz (E-Quartz) countertop market is divided into two main types: artificial and natural. Artificial E-Quartz is manufactured by combining crushed quartz crystals with resin and pigments, resulting in a uniform and customizable appearance. This type is known for its durability, low maintenance, and variety of colors. Conversely, natural E-Quartz is derived from natural quartz stone, maintaining some of its raw characteristics. It typically offers a more organic look and feel while still providing strength and resilience.</p></p>
