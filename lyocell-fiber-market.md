<p><p>Regular Lyocell Fiber is a type of fiber made from natural cellulose found in wood pulp. It is known for its softness, breathability, and absorbency, making it popular in clothing and textile industries. On the other hand, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber undergoes a process where the fibers are chemically treated to enhance their strength, durability, and shrink resistance. This type of fiber is commonly used in products that require more resilience and longevity, such as home textiles and technical textiles.</p></p>
