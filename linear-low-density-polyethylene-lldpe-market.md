<p><p>Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a type of polyethylene with a linear molecular structure, offering enhanced tensile strength and puncture resistance. The market for LLDPE is divided into three main types based on the comonomer used in the production process: C4- LLDPE, C6- LLDPE, and C8- LLDPE. C4-LLDPE is produced using butene as a comonomer, C6-LLDPE is produced using hexene, and C8-LLDPE is produced using octene. Each type offers unique properties and is used in various applications across industries.</p></p>
