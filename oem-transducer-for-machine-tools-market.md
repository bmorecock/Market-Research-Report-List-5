<p><p>OEM transducers for machine tools encompass various types including proximity switches, pressure sensors, temperature sensors, and level sensors. Proximity switches detect the presence or absence of an object without physical contact, enhancing automation. Pressure sensors monitor fluid or gas pressures, ensuring safety and efficiency in operations. Temperature sensors track thermal conditions to prevent overheating and maintain optimal performance. Level sensors measure the height of materials, facilitating inventory and process control. Other sensors include flow and humidity sensors, broadening application capabilities in machine tooling.</p></p>
