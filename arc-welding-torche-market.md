<p><p>The arc welding torch market primarily consists of two types: air-cooled and water-cooled welding torches. Air-cooled torches rely on the surrounding air for cooling, making them lightweight and portable, ideal for light to medium-duty applications. In contrast, water-cooled torches utilize a water circulation system to dissipate heat, enabling higher performance and extended use in heavy-duty welding tasks. Each type is designed to meet specific operational requirements, providing distinct advantages depending on the welding environment and material being processed.</p></p>
