<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Charging Point Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Charging Pile</li><li>AC Charging Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) charging point market is primarily divided into two types: DC (Direct Current) charging piles and AC (Alternating Current) charging piles. DC charging piles provide rapid charging, delivering higher power levels directly to the vehicle's battery, making them ideal for quick recharges in public stations. Conversely, AC charging piles are more common for home or workplace use, offering lower power levels and requiring vehicle onboard chargers to convert AC to DC for battery storage.</p></p>
