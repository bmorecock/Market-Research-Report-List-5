<p><ul><li>Monocalcium Phosphate (MCP)</li><li>Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP)</li><li>Mono-Dicalcium Phosphate (MDCP)</li><li>Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcium phosphate feed market includes Monocalcium Phosphate (MCP), Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP), Mono-Dicalcium Phosphate (MDCP), and Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP). MCP is highly soluble, providing rapid phosphorus and calcium absorption, ideal for young animals. DCP offers balanced nutrient release, commonly used in poultry and swine diets. MDCP combines MCP and DCP benefits, ensuring efficient nutrient utilization. TCP, less soluble, is used for slow-release phosphorus in ruminants. These products enhance animal growth, bone development, and overall health, catering to diverse livestock nutritional needs.</p></p>
