<p><p>Non-contact rotary position sensors are crucial in applications where precise angle measurements are needed without physical wear. The market is divided into two main types: digital output and analog output sensors. Digital output sensors provide discrete signals, often in binary form, indicating specific positions, while analog output sensors generate continuous voltage or current variations proportional to the angle. Each type serves different needs, with digital outputs ideal for digital systems and analog outputs favored for applications requiring variable signal processing.</p></p>
