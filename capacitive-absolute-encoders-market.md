<p><p>Capacitive absolute encoders are precision devices used to determine angular positions in various applications. The market is segmented into single-turn and multi-turn types. Single-turn encoders measure rotational position within a single 360-degree revolution, offering high accuracy for applications with limited rotation. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track multiple revolutions, providing comprehensive positioning data essential for complex machinery and robotics. Each type serves specific industry needs, with single-turn suited for simpler tasks and multi-turn catering to more demanding rotational requirements.</p></p>
