<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controller</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) controller market consists of two main types: PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and PoE Powered Devices (PD) controllers. PSE controllers are responsible for supplying power through Ethernet cables to connected devices, often found in network switches or injectors. In contrast, PD controllers are integrated into devices like cameras or phones, enabling them to receive and utilize the power supplied via the Ethernet connection. This technology enables efficient power and data transmission over a single cable.</p></p>
