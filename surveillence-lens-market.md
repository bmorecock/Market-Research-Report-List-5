<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the surveillance lens market, C-Mount and CS-Mount are the two primary types of mounting systems used to attach lenses to cameras. C-Mount lenses feature a thread size of 1 inch and a flange distance of 17.5 mm, allowing compatibility with various camera types. In contrast, CS-Mount lenses have a shorter flange distance of 12.5 mm and are typically used for modern compact cameras. The choice between these mounts affects image quality, installation ease, and overall system performance.</p></p>
