<p><p>The metallic paint/coating market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based paints use organic solvents for their formulation, offering durability, smooth finish, and enhanced adhesion, often preferred for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based paints utilize water as a solvent, making them eco-friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), ideal for residential and commercial use due to easier cleanup and reduced health risks. Both types serve diverse applications based on performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
