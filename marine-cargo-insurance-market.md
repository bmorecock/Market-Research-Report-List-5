<p><p>Marine cargo insurance offers different coverage types to cater to various shipping risks. "Free from Particular Average" covers major losses, excluding minor damages, typically used for bulk shipments. "With Particular Average" includes compensation for partial losses, thus providing broader protection. "All Risk" insurance covers virtually all incidents, offering maximum security for cargo, including theft and damage. Finally, "Others" may consist of specialized policies tailored for specific cargo types or unique shipping needs, ensuring flexibility in risk management.</p></p>
