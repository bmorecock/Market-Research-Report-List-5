<p><p>The anhydrous substance diatomite market is categorized into three main types: natural, calcined, and flux calcined. Natural diatomite is unprocessed and retains its original structure, used primarily for filtration and insulation. Calcined diatomite undergoes high-temperature processing, enhancing its porosity and reactivity, suitable for applications in pharmaceuticals and agricultural industries. Flux calcined diatomite is treated with specific additives for specialized qualities, making it ideal for specific industrial applications, such as abrasives and catalysts. Each type serves unique applications based on its properties.</p></p>
