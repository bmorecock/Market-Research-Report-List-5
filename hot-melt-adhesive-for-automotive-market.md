Pressure-sensitive adhesives provide immediate adhesion and ease of application, suitable for interior components and trims. Polyamide adhesives exhibit strong thermal and chemical resistance, ideal for automotive assembly. Hot melt polyurethane delivers superior adhesion and durability, commonly used in sealing and structural applications, providing strength in demanding environments.</p></p>
