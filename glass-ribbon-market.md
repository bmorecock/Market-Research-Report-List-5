<p><ul><li>Single Layer Glass</li><li>Laminated Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Ribbon market consists of two primary types: Single Layer Glass and Laminated Glass. Single Layer Glass is a simple, uncoated glass option typically used in applications requiring transparency and basic protection. In contrast, Laminated Glass is composed of multiple glass layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, enhancing safety, security, and sound insulation. This type is ideal for uses in buildings, vehicles, and protective environments, where durability and resistance to breakage are essential considerations. Both types are integral to modern architectural and automotive designs.</p></p>
