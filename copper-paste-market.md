<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Copper Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Sintered</li><li>Medium Temperature Sintered</li><li>High Temperature Sintered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper paste is commonly categorized into three types based on the sintering temperature required for the application process: Low Temperature Sintered, Medium Temperature Sintered, and High Temperature Sintered. Low Temperature Sintered copper paste is applied at temperatures below 300°C, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Medium Temperature Sintered paste requires sintering temperatures between 300-800°C, offering a balance of conductivity and process flexibility. High Temperature Sintered paste is used in applications where sintering temperatures exceed 800°C, providing high electrical conductivity and durability.</p></p>
