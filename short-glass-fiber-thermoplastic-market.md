<p><p>The Short Glass Fiber Thermoplastic market encompasses various processing techniques, primarily Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves injecting the thermoplastic material into molds to create complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, involves forcing the material through a die to produce continuous shapes, such as tubes or sheets. Both methods leverage the reinforcement provided by short glass fibers, enhancing mechanical properties and thermal stability, making them suitable for automotive, electronics, and consumer goods applications.</p></p>
