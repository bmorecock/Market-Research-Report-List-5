<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Detectors</li><li>Ionization Smoke Detectors</li><li>Combination Smoke Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke detector market is primarily segmented into three types: photoelectric smoke detectors, ionization smoke detectors, and combination smoke detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light sensor to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors utilize radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires, making them quick to respond to larger flames. Combination detectors integrate both technologies, offering enhanced safety by detecting a wide range of fire types, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
