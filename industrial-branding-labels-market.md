<p><p>The industrial branding labels market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Pressure-sensitive labels stick to surfaces without additional adhesive, while glue-applied labels require adhesive for attachment. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels conform tightly to containers, providing a tamper-evident solution. In-mold labels are integrated during the manufacturing process, enhancing durability. Heat transfer labels use heat to bond onto products. Other types include non-shrink wraparound labels, gummed labels that require moisture to adhere, and foam labels offering cushioning properties, catering to diverse branding needs.</p></p>
