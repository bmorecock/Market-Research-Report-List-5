<p><p>OSB sheathing, or oriented strand board, is categorized into four market types based on performance and application suitability. OSB/1 is designed for use in dry conditions, suitable for interior applications. OSB/2 offers moisture resistance for general structural uses. OSB/3 is stronger, intended for use in humid environments, providing enhanced durability. OSB/4 is the highest grade, engineered for structural use in demanding conditions, such as where high strength and moisture resistance are crucial. Each type serves distinct construction needs.</p></p>
