<p><p>The axial bone densitometer market is primarily divided into two types: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and QCT (Quantitative Computed Tomography). DEXA is a widely used technique for assessing bone mineral density, providing precise measurements with low radiation exposure. In contrast, QCT uses advanced imaging technology to offer 3D visualization of bone structures, enabling better assessment of bone quality. These modalities cater to different needs in osteoporosis diagnosis and management, influencing market dynamics and growth potential.</p></p>
